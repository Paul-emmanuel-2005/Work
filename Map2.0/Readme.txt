############################## READ ME BEFORE               #######################


 * First install WampServer or Xampp Panel Control if you're running Windows.
 * Then go to C:\ or your disk drive and find the Xamp or Wamp folder.
 * In the case of Wamp , it will search for a www file, and paste the Map2.0 file.
 * In the case of Xamp , it will look for a file htdocs , labas will paste the Map2.0 file.
 * Launch Xamp or Wamp , then go to your browser and enter “Localhost/phpmyadmin”.  
 * Once on the PhpMyAdmin interface, import the dbdrh database.
 * After importing, enter “Localhost/map2.0/pages/index.html” to preview the app.
